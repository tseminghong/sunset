üî¢ Two's Complement

Convert to Two's Complement:

  1. Convert number to binary
  2. Invert all bits (0‚Üí1, 1‚Üí0)
  3. Add 1 to the result
Example: -5 in 8-bit
5 in binary: 00000101
Invert bits: 11111010
Add 1: 11111011
Result: 11111011 = -5

Reverse (Binary to Decimal):

  1. Check first bit: 1 = negative, 0 = positive
  2. If negative: subtract 1, then invert all bits
  3. Convert to decimal and apply sign

About These Resources

These interactive learning materials are designed to help students prepare for the HPCSS ICT curriculum and DSE examinations. The resources focus on important concepts in computing, algorithms, and problem-solving techniques.

Each resource provides:

  • Interactive visualizations to enhance understanding
  • Step-by-step explanations of algorithms and concepts
  • Practice problems with detailed solutions
  • Exam-style questions with guided walkthroughs

These materials supplement classroom learning and can be used for self-study or revision.
